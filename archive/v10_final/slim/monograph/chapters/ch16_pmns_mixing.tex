\chapter{Neutrino PMNS Mixing}
\label{ch:pmns_mixing}

Neutrino mixing angles emerge from the tetrahedron NOEMA edge ratios in the
\(\{\hat I, \hat M, \hat A\}\) operator space.

\section{Tetrahedron Edge Lengths}

The six edges of the NOEMA tetrahedron are determined by \(L_3\) and
\(L_4\):

\[
\begin{aligned}
e_1 &= \frac{L_4}{L_3} = \frac{2}{7}
     \quad (\hat I-\hat M:\ \text{mass splitting}), \\[4pt]
e_2 &= \frac{L_4}{L_3+L_4} = \frac{2}{9}
     \quad (\hat I-\hat A_\mu:\ \theta_{12}\ \text{mixing}), \\[4pt]
e_3 &= \frac{1}{L_3} = \frac{1}{7}
     \quad (\hat I-\hat A_\tau:\ \theta_{13}\ \text{mixing}), \\[4pt]
e_4 &= \frac{(L_4/L_3)^2}{2} = \frac{2}{49}
     \quad (\hat M-\hat A_\mu:\ \Delta m_{21}^2), \\[4pt]
e_5 &= e_4\,(L_3+L_4+1) = \frac{20}{49}
     \quad (\hat M-\hat A_\tau:\ \Delta m_{31}^2), \\[4pt]
e_6 &= \frac{L_4}{L_3} + \left(\frac{L_4}{L_3}\right)^2 = \frac{18}{49}
     \quad (\hat A_\mu-\hat A_\tau:\ \delta_{CP}).
\end{aligned}
\]

\section{Mixing Angles}

The edge lengths map directly to PMNS parameters:

\[
\begin{aligned}
\sin\theta_{13} &= e_3 = \frac{1}{L_3} = \frac{1}{7} = 0.1429, \\[4pt]
\sin^2\theta_{12} &= e_2 + e_1^2
    = \frac{2}{9} + \left(\frac{2}{7}\right)^2
    = 0.2222 + 0.08163
    = 0.3039, \\[4pt]
\sin^2\theta_{23} &= \frac12 + \frac{L_4}{L_3^2}
    = \frac12 + \frac{2}{49}
    = 0.5 + 0.04082
    = 0.5408, \\[4pt]
\delta_{CP} &= \pi\left(1 + \frac{L_4}{L_3} + \left(\frac{L_4}{L_3}\right)^2\right)
    = \pi\left(1 + \frac{2}{7} + \frac{4}{49}\right) \\[4pt]
    &= \frac{67\pi}{49}
    = 246.1^\circ.
\end{aligned}
\]

\section{Comparison with PDG}

\[
\begin{array}{lccc}
\text{Parameter} & \text{Predicted} & \text{PDG} & \text{Error} \\
\hline
\sin^2\theta_{12} & 0.3039 & 0.307 & -1.0\% \\
\sin^2\theta_{23} & 0.5408 & 0.545 & -0.8\% \\
\sin\theta_{13} & 0.143 & 0.148 & -3.4\% \\
\delta_{CP} & 246.1^\circ & 244^\circ & +0.9\%
\end{array}
\]

All parameters are within \(1.6\sigma\) of the PDG best-fit values.
