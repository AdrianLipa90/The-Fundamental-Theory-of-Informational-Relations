```text

```


```text

```

A useful working candidate is:

```text

OI = ln2 / (24*pi)
```


## Boundary

Information by itself is dimensionless. A dimensional normalizer is required:

- `h f`,
- `kB T`,
- field/vacuum sector scale,
- hadron scale,
- Higgs/Yukawa scale,
- or another derived `E0`.

Without such an `E0`, the formula is structurally meaningful but cannot output joules, eV, MeV, or kg.
