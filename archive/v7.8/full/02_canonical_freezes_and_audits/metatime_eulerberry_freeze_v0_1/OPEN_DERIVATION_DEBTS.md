# Open Derivation Debts after Euler--Berry Freeze v0.1

These are intentionally not canonical yet.

## Debt 1 — Structural operator W_s

Need a precise definition:

\[
\hat W_s:D(\hat W_s)\subset\mathcal H_s\to\mathcal H_s.
\]

Candidate meanings:
- winding operator on the phase fiber;
- Collatz-orbital information number;
- generator of twin-prime sector transition;
- Poincaré disk geodesic winding.

Do not conflate these until one canonical definition is chosen.

## Debt 2 — Collatz rhythm rho_s(k)

Need exact, non-fitted formula for

\[
\rho_s(k),\qquad s=(p,p+2).
\]

Candidate families:

\[
\rho_s(k)=\frac{1}{1+\log(1+C^k(p)+C^k(p+2))},
\]

\[
\rho_s(k)=\frac{|C^k(p+2)-C^k(p)|}{C^k(p+2)+C^k(p)},
\]

\[
\rho_s(k)=\arg z_k/2\pi,
\quad z_k\in\mathbb D.
\]

Need one formula tied to geometry, not chosen by fit.

## Debt 3 — Zeta-polar anchors

Need exact map:

\[
(N,S)\mapsto \zeta(1/2+i\gamma_n)=0.
\]

Open choices:
- first zero only;
- paired zeros \(\pm\gamma_n\);
- polar tower indexed by n;
- spectral boundary condition for spinor closure.

## Debt 4 — Tetrahedral depth to SU(3)_c

Need exact derivation of three color directions from tetrahedral internal frame:

\[
T^4\to T^3_{\mathrm{internal}}\to \mathbf 3_c\to SU(3)_c.
\]

Must not be a handwave. Need representation-level map.

## Debt 5 — Hypercharge from closure

Need recover exactly:

\[
Y(Q_L)=1/6,\quad Y(u^c_L)=-2/3,\quad Y(d^c_L)=1/3,
\]

\[
Y(L_L)=-1/2,\quad Y(e^c_L)=1.
\]

Then anomaly cancellation must follow.

## Debt 6 — EB action and mass hierarchy

Need derive \(\mathcal S_f^{EB}\) such that

\[
y_f^{eff}\sim\exp(-\mathcal S_f^{EB}/\kappa)
\]

without importing PDG masses as inputs.

The inverse targets are known, but targets are not derivations.
