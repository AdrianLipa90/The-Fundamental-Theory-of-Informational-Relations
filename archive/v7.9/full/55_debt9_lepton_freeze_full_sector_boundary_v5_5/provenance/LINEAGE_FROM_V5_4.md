# Lineage from v5.4 to v5.5

v5.4 produced the strongest charged-lepton diagnostic so far, but it was still blocked by the inherited electron unit anchor and post-v5.3 residual origin.

v5.5 does not tune the formula. It freezes the v5.4 lepton component split and performs a boundary probe: what happens if the same component gate is reused as a universal charged-fermion gate?

The answer is negative: the lepton formula should not be promoted as a universal fermion operator. Quark sectors require a sector-specific normalization/lift.
