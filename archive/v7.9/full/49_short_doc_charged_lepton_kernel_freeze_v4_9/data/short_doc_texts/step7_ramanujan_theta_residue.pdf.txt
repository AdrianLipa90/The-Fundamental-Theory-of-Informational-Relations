                                                   Abstract
           This note continues the Bloch–Fubini–Study and block-action normal form developed
       in Steps 1–6. Step 6 reduced accelerated Collatz dynamics to a threshold problem over
       valuation words. Step 7 introduces a Ramanujan–theta residue encoding of those words. The
       central point is not that Ramanujan’s formulae prove Collatz, but that they provide the
       correct geometric language for combining three structures already present in the problem:
       residue classes, quadratic deviations from the critical balance 2B ≈ 3k , and the modular
       phase q = e2πiτ . In this language, the number 24 appears through the eta/discriminant
       closure, while π appears through modular phase geometry. This supplies a second, modular
       reading of the normalizer ln 2/(24π).

Conjecture 1 (No infinite non-compensating theta stream). There is no positive odd integer

                                         (2Bk − 3k )m0 > Ck .

7    Residue Classes and Modular Encoding
For each finite valuation word wk , Step 6 gives the residue class of starting values realizing that
word:
                              m0 ≡ (2Bk − Ck )3−k (mod 2Bk +1 ).
    Therefore, finite words are local residue objects. Since every finite positive valuation word
can be realized by infinitely many odd starts, local exclusion is impossible. The obstruction, if it
exists, must be global: an infinite non-compensating word may exist as a 2-adic object but fail
to correspond to a positive ordinary integer.
    The Ramanujan–theta strategy is to encode these residue classes by a q-series whose exponents
measure their distance from the critical balance and whose coefficients count or weight admissible
residue classes.

9      Interpretation
The mathematical picture after Step 7 is the following.

    (i) Collatz valuation words are residue-class objects.

 (ii) Descent is governed by the threshold inequality

                                             (2Bk − 3k )m0 > Ck .

 (iii) The critical balance is
                                              Bk − k log2 3 = 0.

 (iv) Hard prefixes cluster near this balance.

 (v) Ramanujan–theta geometry supplies a natural way to encode squared distance from this
     balance.

 (vi) Eta/discriminant modular closure supplies a second structural reading of the 24 in 24π.

  Thus Ramanujan enters not as decoration, but because the obstruction has become a
modular-residue problem with a quadratic critical defect.

10      Next Step
The next step should not be a further heuristic layer. It should be a precise search for a modular
obstruction:

      Can an infinite non-compensating valuation word define a positive
      integer, or only a nonstandard/2-adic object?

     Equivalently, one must test whether the nested residue classes

                                       rwk   (mod 2Bk +1 )

associated with an indefinitely non-compensating theta stream can have a positive integer
representative.
   If this can be ruled out, the finite compensation theorem follows. If it cannot, the Ramanujan–
theta encoding remains a useful diagnostic, but not a proof mechanism.
