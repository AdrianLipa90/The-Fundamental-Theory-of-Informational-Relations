# DEF-0003 Potential

Status: defined

Symbol: Π

## Role
Potential is the ordered capacity of a system or region to undergo structured reconfiguration under a declared relational frame.

## Formal definition
Potential is the configuration-bearing capacity whose gradients or asymmetries determine admissible tendencies of transformation, reorganization, or stabilization.
