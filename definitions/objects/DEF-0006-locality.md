# DEF-0006 Locality

Status: defined

Symbol: L

## Role
Locality is the constrained regime within which state updates, comparisons, and stabilizations are treated as immediately coupled.

## Formal definition
Locality is the bounded domain of effective relational coherence defined by a boundary condition such that internal updates remain directly comparable before non-local transport is invoked.

## Limits / non-claims
Locality here is not restricted to geometric proximity. It is a structural coherence domain and may later admit multiple realizations.
