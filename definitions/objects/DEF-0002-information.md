# DEF-0002 Information

Status: defined

Symbol: I

## Role
Information is the distinguishable structural content carried, constrained, or transformed within relational configurations.

## Formal definition
Information is the state-differentiating content that permits nontrivial discrimination between admissible configurations of a system under a declared relational frame.
