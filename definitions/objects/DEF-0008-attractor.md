# DEF-0008 Attractor

Status: defined

Symbol: A

## Role
Attractor is the local node or region of stabilized reorganization around which trajectories, flows, or recurrent updates concentrate.

## Formal definition
Attractor is a stability-organizing object in state-relational space such that admissible trajectories tend to gather, recur, or remain bounded relative to it under the active dynamics of the system.
