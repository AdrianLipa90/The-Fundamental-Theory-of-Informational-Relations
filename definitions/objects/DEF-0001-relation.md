# DEF-0001 Relation

Status: defined

Symbol: R

## Role
Relation is the minimal dependency-bearing connection between distinguishable objects, regions, states, or processes.

## Formal definition
A relation is an ordered or structured association that constrains admissible co-description, interaction, transformation, or comparison between at least two distinguishable terms.
