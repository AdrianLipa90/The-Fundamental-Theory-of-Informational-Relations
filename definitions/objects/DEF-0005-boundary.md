# DEF-0005 Boundary

Status: defined

Symbol: ∂

## Role
Boundary is the condition of distinguishability that separates one local regime, state, or region from another.

## Formal definition
Boundary is the minimal structural condition under which a system admits a nontrivial distinction between interior and exterior, local and non-local, or one admissible regime and another.
