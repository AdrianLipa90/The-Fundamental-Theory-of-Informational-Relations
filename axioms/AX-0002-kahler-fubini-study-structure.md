# AX-0002 — Kähler Fubini–Study structure

## Status
`axiom`

## Statement
Projective state space carries a compatible Kähler structure with Fubini–Study metric.

\[
(\mathbb P(\mathcal H), g_{FS}, \omega_{FS}, J)
\]

with:
- \(g_{FS}\): metric structure,
- \(\omega_{FS}\): symplectic form,
- \(J\): complex structure.

## Purpose
This axiom fixes the minimal geometric environment in which transport, phase, and spinorial closure will later be derived.

## Immediate consequences
1. Distances between rays are geometric rather than purely coordinate choices.
2. Closed cycles admit geometric phase accumulation.
3. The minimal two-level sector may be expressed through \(\mathbb{CP}^1\) with induced Fubini–Study structure.
