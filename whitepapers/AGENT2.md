# AGENT2.md

## Local role
AGENT2 maintains cross-reference integrity for the `whitepapers/` layer.

## Scope
- ensure each whitepaper sector has a local `index.md`,
- ensure whitepaper entries link upward to global navigation,
- ensure whitepaper sectors link sideways to derivations and registries,
- ensure no whitepaper silently replaces a derivation or registry.

## Duty boundary
Whitepapers are explanatory and synthesis text only.
AGENT2 must flag any case where a whitepaper introduces an unregistered object without upstream formal placement.

## Required links per whitepaper sector
1. local sector index,
2. global index,
3. at least one derivation or formal object,
4. at least one registry or interface reference when relevant.
