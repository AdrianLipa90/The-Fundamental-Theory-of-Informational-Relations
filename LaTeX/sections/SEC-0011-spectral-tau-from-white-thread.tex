\section{Spectral Tau from Effective White-Thread}
\label{sec:spectral-tau-from-white-thread}

\subsection{Hermitian coupling projection}
Define the projected coupling by
\begin{equation}
A_{ij} = \frac{1}{2}\left(W^{eff}_{ij}+\overline{W^{eff}_{ji}}\right).
\end{equation}
In the symmetric toy sector this reduces to
\begin{equation}
A_{ij}=\Re(W^{eff}_{ij}).
\end{equation}

\subsection{Spectral tau modes}
Given the real symmetric coupling matrix $A$, define
\begin{equation}
\tau_i = \lambda_i(A).
\end{equation}
Thus the first spectral bridge is
\begin{equation}
W^{eff}_{ij} \to A_{ij} \to \tau_i.
\end{equation}

\subsection{Status}
This section records the first spectral bridge at toy-model level only. Flavor/color identification and calibrated fermion masses remain open.
